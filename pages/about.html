About Houdini's House

Gold Coast's most immersive escape room experience.

Our Story

Houdini's House was born from a passion for mystery, puzzles, and immersive storytelling. Founded in 2018 by escape room enthusiasts, our experiences blend theatrical elements with innovative technology to create adventures that transport players to different worlds.

Named after the legendary escape artist Harry Houdini, our rooms are designed to challenge your mind, spark your imagination, and create memorable moments with friends, family, and colleagues.

Our Mission

We believe that the best experiences are the ones that fully immerse you in another world. Our mission is to create escape rooms that don't just test your problem-solving skills but tell compelling stories that make you forget the outside world for 60 minutes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit.

Do I need to book in advance?
Yes, we strongly recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance, especially for weekend sessions.
How many people can play?
Our rooms are designed for 2-6 players. For the best experience, we recommend 4-5 players per room.
Is there an age requirement?
Most of our rooms are suitable for ages 12 and up. Players under 16 must be accompanied by an adult.
What if we get stuck?
Don't worry! Our game masters monitor your progress and can provide hints if you request them.
