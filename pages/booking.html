Booking FAQs

Common questions about booking your escape room experience.

What happens after I book?
After completing your booking, you'll receive a confirmation email with details of your reservation. Please arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled time for a brief introduction.
What's your cancellation policy?
You can reschedule or cancel your booking up to 24 hours before your scheduled time without any penalty. For cancellations with less than 24 hours notice, a 50% fee applies.
Do I need to book in advance?
We strongly recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance, especially for weekend sessions. Walk-ins are accommodated based on availability, but we cannot guarantee spaces without a reservation.
