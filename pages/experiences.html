Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our special experiences

How far in advance should I book a special experience?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance for special experiences, especially during weekends and holiday periods. Corporate events should be booked 4-6 weeks in advance to ensure availability and allow time for customization.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions for catered events?

Yes! Our catering partners offer vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other options for dietary restrictions. Please let us know your requirements when booking so we can ensure everyone is accommodated.

Is there an age limit for birthday parties?

We offer age-appropriate experiences for children as young as 8 years old. For younger children (8-12), we modify our puzzles and provide additional hints. Teenagers and adults can enjoy our standard rooms with varying difficulty levels.

What's included in the Corporate Team Building package?

Our Corporate packages include private room bookings, a dedicated host, pre-game team assessment, the escape room experience, a facilitated debrief session, team photos, access to our meeting space, and basic refreshments. Premium packages include full catering, custom puzzles related to your industry, and detailed team performance reports.
