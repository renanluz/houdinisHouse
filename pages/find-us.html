Getting Here

Multiple convenient ways to reach Houdini's House

By Car

Free parking is available in our dedicated lot. Additional street parking is available on Escape Street and surrounding areas.

From Gold Coast Highway, turn onto Enderley Avenue, then right onto Garfield Terrace. We're located 200m down on the right side.

Public Transport

The 700, 704, and 705 buses stop within a 5-minute walk of our location.

From Surfers Paradise tram station, it's a pleasant 10-minute walk along the beachfront to reach us.

Walking

We're just a 15-minute walk from the heart of Surfers Paradise.

Follow the beachfront path north until you reach Enderley Avenue, then turn left and walk two blocks to find us on Escape Street.

While You're Here

Make a day of it with these nearby attractions

Dining Options

Several excellent restaurants and cafes are within walking distance:

  • The Escape Café – directly across the street
  • Puzzle & Plates – gourmet dining, 5-minute walk
  • Beachfront Bistro – casual dining with ocean views

Attractions

Combine your escape room adventure with these nearby activities:

  • Surfers Paradise Beach – 5-minute walk
  • Ripley's Believe It or Not – 15-minute walk
  • SkyPoint Observation Deck – 20-minute walk

Accommodation

Staying overnight? These hotels are conveniently located:

  • Escape Hotel – next door to us
  • Beachfront Apartments – 5-minute walk
  • Surfers Paradise Marriott – 10-minute drive
