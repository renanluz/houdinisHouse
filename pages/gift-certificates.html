Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our gift certificates

How long are gift certificates valid?
Our gift certificates are valid for 12 months from the date of purchase, giving recipients plenty of time to plan their escape room adventure.
Can I get a physical gift card?
Yes! You can choose between a digital certificate delivered via email or a physical gift card. Physical cards can be picked up at our location or mailed for an additional $5 shipping fee.
How do recipients redeem their gift?
Each gift certificate includes a unique code. Recipients can use this code when booking online through our website or mention it when calling to make a reservation.
Can gift certificates be used for any room?
Yes! Our gift certificates can be used for any of our escape rooms, subject to availability. Recipients can choose whichever room interests them most.
What if the gift amount doesn't cover the full cost?
If a gift certificate doesn't cover the full cost of the chosen experience, recipients can pay the difference at the time of booking. Similarly, if there's remaining value after booking, it can be used for future visits or merchandise.
