Shipping & Returns

Information about our shipping and return policies

Shipping Policy

We ship to most countries worldwide. Standard shipping within Australia takes 3-5 business days. International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days.

Free shipping on all orders over $50 within Australia. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on location and package weight.

Return Policy

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for items in original, unused condition with tags attached. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item was defective or shipped incorrectly.

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team with your order number and reason for return.
