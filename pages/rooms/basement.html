Your Mission: Escape The Basement

You and your friends decided to explore an abandoned house rumored to be haunted. While investigating the basement, the door slams shut behind you, locking you in. Strange noises and eerie whispers fill the air as you realize you're not alone. You must solve the mysteries of the basement and find a way out before whatever lurks in the shadows finds you.

The basement is filled with antique furniture, old photographs, and mysterious artifacts. As you explore, you'll uncover the dark history of the house and its former occupants. Each clue brings you closer to escape, but also deeper into the haunting story of what happened here.

Work together to decipher cryptic messages, unlock hidden compartments, and piece together the tragic events that led to the haunting. But be careful - the entity that resides here doesn't want you to leave.

The Experience

The Basement offers our most atmospheric and immersive experience yet. The room features authentic vintage props, realistic sound effects, and subtle lighting changes that respond to your progress. The puzzles blend logical thinking with narrative discovery, creating a deeply engaging experience.

You'll need to search thoroughly, communicate clearly, and think creatively to escape. The room incorporates elements of mystery, light horror, and historical investigation, making it perfect for thrill-seekers and puzzle enthusiasts alike.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking The Basement experience.

How scary is this room?
The Basement features moderate scare elements including atmospheric lighting, sound effects, and some startling moments. It's designed to be suspenseful and eerie rather than terrifying. If you enjoy haunted houses or thriller movies, you'll likely enjoy this experience.
Is this room suitable for children?
Due to the theme and scare elements, we recommend The Basement for ages 14 and up. Players under 16 must be accompanied by an adult.
What is the recommended team size?
The Basement works best with 3-5 players. The minimum is 2 players, and the maximum is 6. The room has enough puzzles to keep everyone engaged.
