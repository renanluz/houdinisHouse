Your Mission: Find The Cure

A deadly virus has broken out, threatening the entire world population. Your team of elite scientists has tracked down a potential cure to an abandoned research facility, but the lead scientist mysteriously disappeared after becoming infected. You must enter the lab, navigate through various medical puzzles and challenges, find the cure, and escape before you too become infected.

As you enter the facility, you'll find yourself surrounded by medical equipment, cryptic research notes, and puzzling medical samples. The facility's systems are in lockdown, and you'll need to restore power, decode security measures, and piece together the research to synthesize the cure.

Be careful - the facility is equipped with biohazard protocols that will activate if you make too many mistakes. Work together, think critically, and communicate effectively. The world is counting on you!

The Experience

Step into a meticulously designed research laboratory with authentic medical equipment, realistic props, and atmospheric lighting. The Cure provides a deeply immersive experience that will make you feel like you're actually racing against time to save humanity.

The room features a mix of mechanical puzzles, logical deductions, hidden objects, and collaborative challenges. You'll need to work as a team to identify important clues, connect research findings, and ultimately synthesize the cure.

Our state-of-the-art technology enhances the immersion with reactive lighting, sound effects that respond to your actions, and unexpected surprises that will keep your team on edge throughout the experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking The Cure experience.

Do we need medical knowledge to solve the puzzles?
No medical knowledge is required! While the room has a medical theme, all puzzles are designed to be solved using logic, observation, and teamwork. Any necessary "medical" information will be provided within the room.
Is this room scary?
The Cure has some tense moments and atmospheric effects, but it's not designed to be a "scary" experience. It's more about the mystery and the thrill of solving puzzles under time pressure.
What is the recommended team size?
We recommend 4-5 players for the optimal experience. The minimum is 2 players, and the maximum is 6. Larger teams will have plenty to work on simultaneously, while smaller teams might face a greater challenge.
