The Experience

Fenn's Gold combines elements of treasure hunting, adventure, and exploration into an immersive escape room experience. The room features authentic-looking maps, journals, exploration equipment, and a series of interconnected puzzles that recreate the thrill of a real treasure hunt.

You'll need to interpret maps, solve geographical riddles, decipher codes, and piece together fragments of information to progress. The puzzles balance logical thinking with creative interpretation, making this room accessible to both newcomers and experienced escapees.

With an engaging storyline inspired by real-world treasure hunts and puzzles that capture the essence of adventure, Fenn's Gold offers an experience that will appeal to the explorer in everyone.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking the Fenn's Gold experience.

Is this room good for families with children?
Yes! Fenn's Gold is one of our more family-friendly rooms. The adventure theme and treasure hunt concept appeal to children, and the puzzles can be solved by mixed-age groups working together. We recommend it for families with children aged 10 and up.
Do we need geographical knowledge to solve the puzzles?
No specific geographical knowledge is required. While the room includes maps and location-based puzzles, all the information you need to solve them is provided within the room. Basic map-reading skills may be helpful but are not necessary.
What makes this room different from your others?
Fenn's Gold has a stronger emphasis on adventure and exploration than our other rooms. It features more physical interaction with the environment, map-based puzzles, and a progressive discovery path that mimics a real treasure hunt. The room is designed to evoke the feeling of being on an expedition.
