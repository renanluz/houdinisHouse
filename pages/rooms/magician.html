Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking the Missing Magician experience.

Do I need to know anything about magic to enjoy this room?
No magical knowledge is required! While the room celebrates the world of illusion, all puzzles can be solved with observation, teamwork, and reasoning. That said, magic enthusiasts will appreciate the authentic details and references throughout the room.
Is this room scary?
The Missing Magician is mysterious and atmospheric rather than scary. There are moments of surprise and wonder, but it's not designed to frighten. The focus is on the intrigue of the storyline and the cleverness of the puzzles.
Are there any special effects I should be aware of?
The room features theatrical lighting, occasional small smoke effects, and some surprising mechanical movements. Nothing is intended to startle, but rather to create a sense of wonder and amazement that fits with the magical theme.
