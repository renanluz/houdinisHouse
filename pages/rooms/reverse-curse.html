Your Mission: Break the Ancient Curse

You and your team are paranormal investigators called to an old Victorian mansion with a dark history. The current owner, a collector of occult artifacts, has unwittingly activated an ancient curse after acquiring a mysterious relic. Strange occurrences have plagued the house, and the owner has mysteriously vanished.

Upon entering the house's hidden occult chamber, you discover the owner's journal detailing their final desperate attempts to reverse the curse. The door slams shut behind you, and paranormal activity intensifies. You have 60 minutes to decipher arcane symbols, locate the cursed artifact, and perform the ritual to break the curse‚Äîbefore you become its next victims.

The answers lie in ancient texts, mystical symbols, and the forgotten history of the house itself. Can you unravel the supernatural mystery and escape the dark forces that have been unleashed?

The Experience

Reverse the Curse offers our most atmospheric and immersive supernatural experience. The room features authentic Victorian decor, mystical artifacts, and special effects that create a genuinely eerie environment. Subtle lighting and sound effects enhance the supernatural ambiance without relying on cheap scares.

The puzzles blend logical problem-solving with intuitive connections, requiring you to piece together fragmented information across different mystical disciplines. You'll decode ancient symbols, reconstruct ritual components, and trace the history of the curse through hidden clues throughout the room.

While the room has supernatural themes and an appropriately eerie atmosphere, it focuses on mystery and discovery rather than horror. It's ideal for those who enjoy supernatural themes and atmospheric storytelling combined with challenging puzzles.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking the Reverse the Curse experience.

Is this room scary? Will there be jump scares?
Reverse the Curse creates an eerie and atmospheric experience but is not designed to be frightening. There are no jump scares or actors. The room relies on ambient lighting, subtle sound effects, and immersive storytelling to create a supernatural atmosphere rather than horror elements.
Is this room suitable for teens?
Yes, we recommend this room for ages 13 and up when accompanied by an adult. While the supernatural themes and atmospheric elements create tension, there is nothing graphic or intensely frightening that would be inappropriate for teenagers.
Do we need knowledge of the occult or supernatural to solve the puzzles?
No prior knowledge is required. All the information you need to solve the puzzles and reverse the curse is provided within the room. The puzzles involve symbols, codes, and connections that can be solved with logical thinking and careful observation.
