The Experience

The Study offers our most intellectually challenging escape room experience. The room features authentic academic decor, antique books, mysterious artifacts, and puzzles that will challenge even the most experienced escapees.

You'll need to apply principles from history, archaeology, mathematics, and linguistics to solve complex riddles and codes. The room progresses through a series of interconnected puzzles that gradually reveal the professor's discoveries and the danger they've placed him in.

With multiple layers of mystery to unravel and our most intricate puzzle design yet, The Study is perfect for experienced escape room enthusiasts looking for a serious challenge to their deductive reasoning and problem-solving abilities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking The Study experience.

How difficult is this room compared to your others?
The Study is our most intellectually challenging room. It features complex puzzles that require critical thinking, attention to detail, and the ability to connect multiple clues. We recommend it for experienced escape room enthusiasts or teams with strong problem-solving skills.
Do we need special knowledge to solve the puzzles?
No specialized academic knowledge is required. While the room has themes of archaeology, history, and cryptography, all necessary information is provided within the room. However, a general knowledge of history and good pattern recognition skills will be advantageous.
What is the recommended team size for The Study?
The Study works best with 4-5 players. The minimum is 2 players, and the maximum is 6. Due to the complexity and quantity of puzzles, having more team members can help distribute the mental workload.
